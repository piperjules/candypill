Digital Alchemy

Tactile Interface

2023

A square mesh fabric installation featuring a photographic image of a hand reaching through a translucent surface. The piece explores themes of touch, boundary, and digital-physical interfaces through its material qualities and imagery. The white mesh fabric creates a screen-like effect that both reveals and obscures the image, inviting viewers to consider how we navigate between tangible and intangible realms.

  • Medium: Photographic print on mesh fabric
  • Dimensions: 24 × 24 inches
