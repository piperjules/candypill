Digital Alchemy

Fragmented Memory

2023

A layered mixed media work on wood panel featuring collaged photographic elements in muted tones of beige, orange, and red. The piece presents fragmented imagery that appears to be from vintage photographs or printed materials, creating a palimpsest of memory and time. The weathered surface and visible textures suggest the erosion and transformation of personal and collective histories.

  • Medium: Mixed media collage on wood panel
  • Dimensions: 20 × 20 inches
