Mountain Portal

2023

A vertical hanging textile piece featuring a photographic landscape printed on translucent fabric. The image depicts a serene mountain vista with architectural elements in the foreground, creating a window-like portal to another place and time. The gentle draping of the fabric and its translucent quality transform the photographic image into something more ethereal and dreamlike, blurring the boundary between documentation and imagination.

  • Medium: Photographic print on translucent fabric
  • Dimensions: 48 × 24 inches
