Wooden Crown

2023

A portrait transferred onto wood, where the natural grain becomes part of the composition. The figure wears a dark crown atop vibrant orange-red hair, creating a striking contrast against the warm tones of the wood. This piece explores the intersection of traditional portraiture and organic materials.

  • Medium: Photographic transfer on wood
  • Dimensions: 18 × 24 inches
