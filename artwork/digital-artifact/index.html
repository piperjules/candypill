Digital Alchemy

Digital Artifact

2023

A geometric abstraction on wood panel featuring a central red-orange form that appears both digital and organic in nature. The pixelated, glitch-like quality of the central element contrasts with the warm, natural tone of the wooden background, creating a dialogue between digital processes and traditional materials. The piece explores how digital information manifests in physical space.

  • Medium: Digital transfer on wood panel
  • Dimensions: 18 × 18 inches
