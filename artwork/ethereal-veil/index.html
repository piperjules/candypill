Ethereal Veil

2023

A sculptural fabric installation featuring a large, draped translucent material with subtle photographic imagery visible through its surface. The piece stands tall with elegant folds that create a sense of movement and presence. Soft lavender, beige, and gray tones blend together in a dreamlike composition that shifts as light passes through the material. This work explores themes of memory, perception, and the ephemeral nature of experience through its material qualities and ghostly imagery.

  • Medium: Photographic transfer on silk organza
  • Dimensions: 72 × 48 × 24 inches
