Portals

Twilight Passages

2023

A textile artwork with a deep indigo background illuminated by vertical columns of light. The glowing orange and yellow elements emerge from the darkness like doorways or passages to another dimension. The piece evokes the liminal space between day and night, or perhaps between different states of consciousness. The rich purple tones and luminous accents create a meditative, almost mystical atmosphere that invites contemplation of thresholds and transitions.

  • Medium: Dye and pigment on linen
  • Dimensions: 28 × 18 inches
