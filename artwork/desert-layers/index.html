Desert Layers

2023

A digital exploration of dimensional space using a desert landscape as canvas. Multiple rectangular frames create a portal-like effect, inviting the viewer to step through layers of reality. The vibrant blue sky contrasts with the soft beige sand dunes and sparse vegetation, suggesting an oasis of possibility within the arid environment.
