Portals

Verdant Passage

2023

A textile artwork featuring flowing gradients of green, blue, and purple on a delicate fabric. The piece creates a sense of movement through its organic patterns and color transitions, suggesting a journey through natural landscapes. The draped, undulating form of the fabric adds a dimensional quality that transforms the flat image into a sculptural presence, inviting viewers to imagine stepping through this portal into a lush, vibrant environment.

  • Medium: Hand-dyed silk
  • Dimensions: 24 × 24 inches
