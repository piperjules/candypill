Portals

Shoreline Memory

2023

A delicate translucent fabric artwork capturing the essence of a coastal landscape. The crumpled, organic form creates a three-dimensional topography where the upper portion fades to ethereal white while the lower section reveals rich detail of vegetation, stones, and aquatic elements. This piece explores the fragility of environmental memory and the layered experience of natural spaces through its materiality and photographic transfer technique.

  • Medium: Photographic transfer on handmade paper
  • Dimensions: 30 × 16 inches
