Play on Space

Translucent Prisms

2023

A diptych photographic study of colorful translucent objects arranged against a white background. The vibrant orange, blue, purple, and amber forms create a play of light, color, and transparency as they overlap and interact. The composition explores how light passes through these geometric forms, creating new colors and shapes at their intersections. This piece invites contemplation of perception, materiality, and the transformative nature of light.

  • Medium: Digital photography
  • Dimensions: 24 × 36 inches
