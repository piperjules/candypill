Digital Alchemy

Memory Fragments

2023

A sculptural textile installation featuring translucent mesh fabric arranged in an angular, three-dimensional form. The piece contains collaged photographic elements and printed materials visible through the mesh, creating layers of visual information that shift as the viewer moves around the work. This exploration of memory and documentation examines how information is filtered, fragmented, and reconstructed through both physical and mental processes.

  • Medium: Photographic transfer on mesh fabric
  • Dimensions: 30 × 24 × 12 inches
