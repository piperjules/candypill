Digital Alchemy

Embodied Memory

2023

A textile artwork printed on translucent fabric featuring a figure with vibrant red hair in blue clothing, overlaid with green plant elements. The piece explores the relationship between the human form and natural environment through layered imagery that creates a dreamlike, ethereal quality. The translucent material allows light to pass through, creating different viewing experiences as lighting conditions change.

  • Medium: Digital print on silk organza
  • Dimensions: 36 × 24 inches
