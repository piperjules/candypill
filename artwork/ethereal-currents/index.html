Digital Alchemy

Ethereal Currents

2023

A translucent fabric artwork with vibrant red and teal elements against a silvery background. The piece features organic flowing lines and abstract forms that create a sense of movement across the delicate surface. The frayed edges and visible mounting points emphasize the material nature of the work, while the composition suggests a dialogue between structure and fluidity.

  • Medium: Mixed media on translucent fabric
  • Dimensions: 24 × 18 inches
