Play on Space

Chromatic Flow

2023

A vibrant abstract composition featuring fluid, swirling patterns in orange, yellow, turquoise, and white. The piece captures a sense of dynamic movement through its organic forms and bold color transitions. The deckled edges of the paper add a tactile quality to this exploration of color interaction and visual rhythm, suggesting both natural phenomena like water currents and the more ephemeral flow of emotions or energy.

  • Medium: Fluid acrylic on handmade paper
  • Dimensions: 18 × 24 inches
