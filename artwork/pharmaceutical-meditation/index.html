Digital Alchemy

Pharmaceutical Meditation

2023

A mixed media piece featuring orange and white pill capsules arranged in a circular pattern on a textured, dark surface with a soft pink border. The work explores themes of medication, consumption, and the intersection of health and art through the transformation of everyday pharmaceuticals into aesthetic objects.

  • Medium: Mixed media with pharmaceutical elements on panel
  • Dimensions: 18 × 18 inches
