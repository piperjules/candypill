Portals

Aquatic Topography

2023

A sculptural relief piece in deep blue that captures the undulating patterns of water in three dimensions. The work transforms fluid movement into a solid form, freezing a moment of wave motion into a tactile landscape that invites both visual and physical exploration. The monochromatic blue surface creates a meditative quality, while the rhythmic ripples evoke the constant motion of oceanic currents and tidal patterns.

  • Medium: Molded resin with pigment
  • Dimensions: 36 × 36 × 4 inches
