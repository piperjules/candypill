Portals

Crystalline Form

2023

A sculptural piece that blurs the boundary between natural and artificial materiality. The vibrant turquoise blue form, veined with intricate black patterns, resembles both a precious mineral and an otherworldly artifact. Its glossy surface captures and refracts light, creating an interplay between transparency and opacity. This work explores themes of geological time, transformation, and the uncanny valley between organic growth and artistic intervention.

  • Medium: Resin, pigment, and mixed media
  • Dimensions: 12 × 8 × 6 inches
