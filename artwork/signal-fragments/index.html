Digital Alchemy

Signal Fragments

2023

A vibrant textile artwork in rich crimson and black tones featuring geometric patterns and white graphic elements. The piece explores themes of communication, technology, and information through its visual language of circuit-like lines and abstract symbols. The draped fabric creates dimensional folds that transform the flat imagery into a sculptural form, suggesting the malleability of digital information as it moves through physical space.

  • Medium: Digital print on satin fabric
  • Dimensions: 30 × 30 inches
