Wind Through Grass

2023

A textile artwork capturing the ephemeral quality of movement through a landscape. The diagonal streaks of green, yellow, and deep purple create a sense of motion, as if the viewer is passing through a field at speed or witnessing grass bending in a strong breeze. The intentional blurring technique transforms a static natural scene into a dynamic experience, exploring the intersection of time, movement, and perception.

  • Medium: Dye on silk
  • Dimensions: 24 × 16 inches
